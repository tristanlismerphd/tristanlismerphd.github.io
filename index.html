Research

What I work on.

01 / EXPERIMENTAL

Testing Born's Rule

Designed and built a 4-slit photonic experiment to measure the Sorkin parameter I₃ — directly probing whether quantum interference is second-order. A non-zero result would require rewriting the foundations of quantum mechanics.

Quantum Foundations
02 / COMPUTATIONAL

GPT Rank Fitting

Cross-validated ALS rank-sweep pipeline extracting the rank of experimental interference matrices — characterising the GPT state space directly from data, without assuming the theory upfront.

Data Analysis · State Space Theory
03 / THEORETICAL

Epistemic Restrictions in GPTs

Knowledge-balance principles in Generalised Probabilistic Theories. Novel Trichotomy Theorem: a complete characterisation of which GPTs admit an epistemic interpretation.

GPT Framework
04 / EXPERIMENTAL

Interferometer Expertise

Precision alignment of HOM, Mach-Zehnder, and Sagnac interferometers. SNSPDs, APDs, ICCD cameras, Ti:Sapphire at 790 nm, Sagnac SPDC entangled photon sources.

Photonics · Optics
