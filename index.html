Tristan Lismer

PhD Candidate in Quantum Optics | Institute for Quantum Computing

I am a PhD candidate at the Institute for Quantum Computing in Waterloo. My research focuses on experimental tests of quantum foundations, generalized probabilistic theories, and higher-order interference using photonic systems.

I work jointly with Kevin Resch and Rob Spekkens.

Current Focus

  • Experimental tests of quantum mechanical axioms
  • Three-slit interference experiments
  • Generalized probability theory frameworks
  • Photonic qubit state preparation and measurement
