Research

What I work on.

01

Testing Born's Rule

Designed and built a 4-slit photonic experiment to measure the Sorkin parameter I₃ — directly probing whether quantum interference is second-order. A non-zero result would require rewriting the foundations of quantum mechanics.

Experimental · Quantum Foundations
02

GPT Rank Fitting

Cross-validated ALS rank-sweep pipeline extracting the rank of experimental interference matrices — characterising the GPT state space directly from data, without assuming the theory upfront.

Data Analysis · State Space Theory
03

Epistemic Restrictions in GPTs

Theoretical work on knowledge-balance principles in Generalised Probabilistic Theories. Novel Trichotomy Theorem: a complete characterisation of which GPTs admit an epistemic interpretation.

Theory · GPT Framework
04

Interferometer Expertise

Precision alignment of HOM, Mach-Zehnder, and Sagnac interferometers. SNSPDs, APDs, ICCD cameras, Ti:Sapphire at 790 nm, Sagnac SPDC entangled photon sources. Years of hands-on optical lab expertise.

Experimental · Photonics
