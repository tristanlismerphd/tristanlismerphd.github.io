// About

WHO
I
AM

Fourth-year PhD candidate at the Institute for Quantum Computing, co-supervised by Kevin Resch and Rob Spekkens (Perimeter Institute).

I do both sides of the work — I derive the theory, then I go into the lab and test it. My goal is to find out whether the rules we think govern reality are actually the rules.

Quantum Foundations Interferometry GPT Theory Born's Rule SNSPDs / APDs Python · LabVIEW Mathematica HOM / Mach-Zehnder

The Experiment

Building 3 and 4-slit photonic experiments to measure the Sorkin parameter — directly testing whether quantum probability obeys Born's rule or admits higher-order interference. If the answer is no, physics needs new foundations.

The Analysis

Developed a cross-validated GPT rank-fitting pipeline using ALS matrix factorisation to characterise quantum state spaces from experimental data. Identifies the rank of the measurement matrix directly — no assumptions about the theory needed.

The Lab Craft

Expert in free-space interferometer alignment — HOM, Mach-Zehnder, Sagnac. Fluent with SNSPDs, APDs, ICCD cameras, and Ti:Sapphire systems. LabVIEW for instrument control, Python for analysis, Mathematica for theory.

The Theory

Parallel theoretical work on epistemic restrictions in Generalised Probabilistic Theories. A Trichotomy Theorem characterising which GPTs admit a knowledge-limit interpretation — the kind of result you can prove and also build an experiment around.

Testing Born's Rule

Designed and built a 4-slit photonic experiment to measure the Sorkin parameter I₃, directly probing whether quantum interference is truly second-order. A non-zero result would mean the textbooks are wrong.

02

GPT Rank Fitting

Cross-validated ALS rank-sweep pipeline that extracts the rank of experimental interference matrices across all slit configurations — characterising the GPT state space directly from data, without assuming the theory.

Precision alignment of HOM, Mach-Zehnder, and Sagnac interferometers. SNSPDs, APDs, ICCD cameras, Ti:Sapphire at 790 nm, Sagnac SPDC entangled photon sources. Years of hands-on optical expertise.
